   • MA 20/120: +22% (barely positive)
   • TSMOM 90: -6% (negative)
   • Donchian Breakout: -4.3% (negative)
   → Bear-dominated or choppy trends
   → Pure momentum strategies not working

3. LONGER LOOKBACKS > SHORTER
   • XSMOM 180 (63.4%) > XSMOM 90 (20.3%)
   • 120-day MA (22%) > 60-day MA (theoretical underperformance)
   → Reduces noise, captures true trends
   → Shorter lookbacks too reactive

4. EQUAL-WEIGHT > SCORE-WEIGHTING
   • Binary inclusion (top/low vol): outperforms
   • Score-weighted positioning: underperforms
   → Asset selection > position sizing in this period

5. TRANSACTION COSTS MATTER
   • Low Vol: 11.5 trades (safe from slippage)
   • TSMOM: 69 trades (high cost sensitivity)
   • Assumption: 0.15% per trade = material impact

6. LOW VOL HAS LOWEST DRAWDOWN
   • Persistent Low Vol: -26.8% max DD
   • TSMOM + Low Vol: -32.2% max DD
   • Cross-Sectional: -60.4% max DD
   • Donchian: -68.3% max DD
   → Risk management edge for low-vol approach

7. REGIME PERFORMANCE CONSISTENT
   • Bull: All strategies perform well
   • Sideways: Low-vol and filtered strategies shine
   • Bear: Most strategies suffer, low-vol holds up best
